Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have feedback, found a bug, or just want to say hello, feel free to reach out through any of the channels below.

contact@worldclickers.com

Bug Reports

Found something that doesn't work right? Please include the following when reporting bugs:

  • Your browser name and version
  • Your operating system
  • Transaction ID (for payment issues)
  • A description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new resource, tool, or game feature? We're always looking for ways to make World Clickers better. Send us your suggestions and they might make it into a future update!

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. Thank you for your patience and for being part of the World Clickers community!
