World Clickers is a global idle-clicker and resource-mining game where players generate wealth by clicking, auto-mining, and strategically investing in tools. Each click contributes to a country-based economy, allowing nations to compete for global dominance through collective wealth accumulation.


As players progress, astronauts unlock lunar exploration, opening an entirely new tier of resources with dramatically higher values. The first astronaut from each country to land on the Moon will have their name permanently recorded forever as the first representative of their nation to reach the Moon, creating historic moments, national pride, and true digital legacy.
