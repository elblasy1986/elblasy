Fly to The Moon

World Clickers is a global idle-clicker and resource-mining game where players generate wealth by clicking, auto-mining, and strategically investing in tools. Each click contributes to a country-based economy, allowing nations to compete for global dominance through collective wealth accumulation.


As players progress, astronauts unlock lunar exploration, opening an entirely new tier of resources with dramatically higher values. The first astronaut from each country to land on the Moon will have their name permanently recorded forever as the first representative of their nation to reach the Moon, creating historic moments, national pride, and true digital legacy.

Resources Guide

Wood

Common Earth resource (28% rarity, value 1). The foundation of early progression and basic income.

Fish

Common resource (22% rarity, value 2). Represents oceans and food supply with slightly higher returns than wood.

Coal

Common but strategic (15% rarity, value 4). Powers early industrial progress and steady growth.

Stone

Appears at 10% rarity (value 5). Supports durability, construction, and expansion.

Oil

Uncommon resource (8% rarity, value 10). Introduces modern energy and higher income.

Iron

Uncommon industrial resource (7% rarity, value 15). Key to mid-game manufacturing and strength.

Copper

Rare resource (5% rarity, value 20). Vital for advanced industry and electrical systems.

Silver

Rare precious metal (3% rarity, value 40). Enables high-value wealth and leaderboard growth.

Gold

Very Rare (1.5% rarity, value 80). A symbol of power, prestige, and global wealth.

Diamond

Ultra Rare Earth resource (0.5% rarity, value 150). The peak of Earth mining rewards.

Regolith Dust

Most common lunar resource (35% rarity, value 200). Abundant yet highly valuable.

Lunar Glass

Appears at 25% rarity (value 300). Formed under extreme lunar conditions.

Lunar Ice

Uncommon (15% rarity, value 450). Essential for survival and future colonization.

Lunar Sapphire

Rare (8% rarity, value 700). Highly desired cosmic crystal.

Ancient Meteor Alloy

Rare resource (6% rarity, value 1000). Forged from ancient meteor impacts.

Quantum Fiber

Epic material (4% rarity, value 1500). Introduces advanced futuristic technology.

Stardust Crystals

Epic resource (3% rarity, value 2000). Pure cosmic energy.

Moonstone Core

Legendary (2.5% rarity, value 3000). The heart of lunar power.

Dark Matter Fragments

Mythic resource (1.3% rarity, value 4000). Extremely rare and powerful.

Ethereum Pulse

Ultra Mythic (0.2% rarity, value 5000). The ultimate cosmic resource.
