About

World Clickers is a global idle-clicker and resource-mining game where players generate wealth by clicking, auto-mining, and strategically investing in tools.

As players progress, they unlock lunar and planetary exploration, opening an entirely new tier of resources with dramatically higher values. Land on new planets creating historic moments and a true digital legacy.
