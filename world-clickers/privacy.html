Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

World Clickers ("we", "our", or "the game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you play World Clickers.

Information We Collect

To provide a persistent and secure experience, we collect the following data:

  • Game Data: Your progress, currency, inventory, and leaderboard scores are stored on our secure servers. This allows you to access your game from multiple devices.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase tools, our trusted payment processors collect necessary billing information. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Device & Usage Data: We may collect device identifiers and usage statistics to improve game performance and security.

How We Use Information

  • To synchronize your progress across devices
  • To process payments and deliver purchased content
  • To maintain global leaderboards and country statistics
  • To prevent fraud and ensure fair play

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • ipapi.co: For IP-based country detection. Their privacy policy can be found at ipapi.co/privacy.
  • Google Fonts: For loading web fonts. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Font Awesome: For icon display via CDN.
  • hatscripts circle-flags: For displaying country flag images.

Data Storage

Your game data, account information, and purchase history are stored on secure cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to maintain your active session, secure your account, and analyze game performance. By using our site, you consent to these technologies.

Children's Privacy

World Clickers is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the game requires no registration and collects no personal data, it is safe for players of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
