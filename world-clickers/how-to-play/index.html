How to Play!

The Universe at Your Fingertips

Your journey begins with a single click. Interact with the Earth, the Moon, or any celestial body you've discovered to manually mine resources.

Every click generates immediate wealth, allowing you to kickstart your galactic empire. As you upgrade, your clicks become more powerful, extraction more efficient, and your reach more vast.

Valuable Resources

The universe is rich with diverse materials. From common Earthly resources like Wood and Iron to rare Lunar Dust and planetary gases, each material has its own market value.

Keep an eye on the sidebars to see what you are currently mining. Rare resources found on distant planets are worth significantly more than basic materials found on Earth.

Build Your Empire

You don't have to do it all alone. Visit the Tools Shop in the bottom right corner of your screen to invest in automated equipment.

From Timber Axes and Hydro Netters to massive Crystal Drills, these tools will mine resources for you as long as you are in the game, providing a steady stream of income alongside your clicks.

Feel the Heat

Efficiency is everything. The Heat Bar on the left side of your screen monitors your mining intensity. The faster and more consistently you mine, the higher it climbs.

Reaching maximum heat is the goal for any serious industrialist. Maintaining high energy levels is essential for unlocking the most advanced profit mechanics in the game.

Profit Multipliers

When you push your mining heat to its absolute limit, the Multiplier system in the top right kicks in. This powerful mechanic can double, triple, or even quadruple your total earnings per second!

Maintain your peak performance to keep the multiplier active. The longer you stay at maximum intensity, the more legendary your wealth will become.

Epic Challenges

Ready to put your skills to the test? Take on time-limited missions in the right center of your screen to mine a specific amount of a resource in a row.

Successful completion of these missions grants massive cash rewards. The more advanced your tools, the more valuable the prizes you can win!

Legacy & Glory

Every milestone you reach is recorded in your Achievements. Unlocking these milestones proves your status as the ultimate space industrialist.

Earn enough achievements to unlock permanent Medals. Each medal isn't just for show—it grants a permanent 10% bonus to your total wealth, making every future click and tool even more valuable.

Interplanetary Travel

Earth is only the beginning. Once you've accumulated enough wealth and power, board your rocket and travel to destinations like the Moon, Mars, and beyond.

Each world offers entirely new resources, unique tools, and much higher profit margins. Conquer the entire solar system to become the greatest miner in history.
