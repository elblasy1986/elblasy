Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

World Clickers ("we", "our", or "the game") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the game operates and clarifies that no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted when you play World Clickers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal or gameplay data on our servers.

  • Game Data: All your progress, currency, and inventory exist only locally in your browser using local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Payment Information: There are no real-money purchases in the game. All transactions are done using virtual in-game currency stored locally in your browser.
  • Device & Usage Data: We do not collect device information, analytics, or usage statistics.

How We Use Information

  • We do not collect or use any personal or gameplay information.
  • All game functionality runs locally within your browser.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for visual and interface purposes only:

  • Google Fonts: For loading web fonts. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Font Awesome: For icon display via CDN.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device (browser storage). We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any servers.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. The game may use local storage within your browser solely to save your progress.

Children's Privacy

World Clickers is suitable for all ages. Since no personal data is collected and all data is stored locally on the user's device, the game is safe for children and does not require parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
